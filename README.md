# Startup Intelligence Agent

A 6-step agentic AI system that autonomously researches and delivers 
investor-grade teardowns of Indian startups.

## What it does
Enter any Indian startup name. The agent runs a multi-step pipeline:
1. Entity Recognition
2. Market Research  
3. Competitive Scan
4. Risk Assessment
5. Scoring Engine
6. Verdict Generation

Output: Business model analysis, 4-dimension scoring, competitive 
landscape, top risks, growth opportunities, and a final investor verdict.

## Why I built this
India has 100,000+ startups. Investors, founders, and students have 
no fast way to get structured intelligence on any of them. This agent 
solves that in 15 seconds.
